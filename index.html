A Little Bit About Me

I love solving problems—

Rubik's Cubes, jigsaw puzzles, and inactive wifi routers were just a few of the problems I tinkered with when I was little.

When I arrived at Northwestern in 2021, I took my first computer science class and loved it. I was fascinated by the ability to implement my ideas into code.

Since then, I chose to explore many different applications of CS. I gained UI/UX design experience when designing a new app for 1,000 dancers as part of Northwestern’s Dance Marathon. I learned how to refactor code and build APIs when working for Uniphore, a conversational AI unicorn company. Most recently, I gained experience with frontend development while building an internal testing dashboard for 12,000 engineers at Capital One.

My design philosophy is based around attention to detail and iteration. In every challenge, I bring confidence, determination, and optimism while being friendly and enjoying the journey.

Outside of building tech, I love cooking new recipes, watching sports, and traveling abroad! As I start my career, I’m excited to use technology to create digital experiences that impact on millions of people's worldwide.
