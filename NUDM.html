Northwestern Dance Marathon App

A new app for 1,000 dancers to use throughout the year to fundraise and track events.

Figma Designs

Overview

I worked with four UI/UX Designers on the Tech and Analytics team to design a new app for Northwestern University Dance Marathon (NUDM). Previously, NUDM used a very basic app but it was mainly designed for the 30-hour fundraiser and very few people used it. I helped design a brand new app for dancers to use throughout the year to view individual and team fundraising progress and register for monthly events using a new NUDM calendar.

My Impact

I focused on designing the interface and interactions with the events calendar pages. I created a vertical screen for users to view all upcoming events and filter by events that they have registered for. I also designed individual pages for different types of events as well as the interface for dancers to register for events. Lastly, I integrated functionality for dancers to add NUDM events to their personal calendar apps.

Technologies

Figma, Adobe Illustrator
